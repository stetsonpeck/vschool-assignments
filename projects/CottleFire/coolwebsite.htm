The Fire Protection Contractor of Choice

Fire Engineering provides the highest quality of Fire protection available from Fire Sprinklers, Suppression, Detection and Services. Fire Engineering is one of the few companies that employs Fire Sprinkler, Fire Alarm and Special Hazards NICET Level 4 professionals, which is the highest certification level recognized in the fire protection industry.

Applications

Fire Engineering has been Designing, Fabricating, Installing, Maintaining, and Inspecting Fire Sprinkler, Suppression and Detection systems of all types, Since 1947. Our combined team knowledge and design capability helps us maintain our reputation as a Leader in the Fire Protection Industry and has earned Us recognition as an AFSA Quality Contractor.
